From Tech Camp to the IOL

My first experience at the UNH IOL was through UNH's Tech Camp. Tech Camp is a two week
summer program where middle and high school students are exposed to research being conducted
around the university.

During the first week of the program, students spend half day periods at numerous university
facilities in order to learn about the research conducted at each (14 facilities total).
At the beginning to the second week, each student chooses which research they most preferred
and then spends the remainder of the second week at their chosen research facility.

Unsurprisingly, I chose the UNH IOL, for two years of tech camp in fact. After my third year,
the IOL's program director suggested I should apply for the internship program. And the rest
is history.
This interview details my journey through the above process.
