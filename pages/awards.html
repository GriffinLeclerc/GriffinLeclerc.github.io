Awards

University of New Hampshire Deans List
2017, 2018, 2019, 2020, 2021

Students named to the Dean's List at the University of New Hampshire are students who have earned recognition
through their superior scholastic performance during a semester enrolled in a full-time course load
(12 or more graded credits).

More detail about each award can be found here.

UNH InterOperability Lab Student Leadership Award
2018, 2019

This award was established by the UNH IOL to provide meritorious scholarship support to student employees.
Recipients must have good academic standing and have a demonstrated record of high performance.

New Hampshire Charitable Foundation
William H. Greenough Scholarship
2017, 2018, 2019, 2020, 2021

This Fund is part of the Foundation’s “NH Tomorrow” initiative, providing opportunity scholarships
to promising young people to help them reach their potential and thrive in the workplace.
The grants range between $2500 to $7500 and are renewable annually based upon academic performance.
I have received the maximum allowable award for each year of my four undergraduate years.

UNH College of Engineering and Physical Sciences
Maurice Page Scholarship
2019

This scholarship is awarded annually to one deserving undergraduate
student who resides in New Hampshire.

UNH College of Engineering and Physical Sciences
John B. and Martha M.W. Zocchi Scholarship
2018

The Zocchi’s share a deep commitment to higher education and wish to provide financial support to students
who have the ability to succeed. The scholarship is awarded to talented students to help achieve their dreams.
